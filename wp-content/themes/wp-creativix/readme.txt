Installation:
---------------------------------------------------

- Upload wp-creativix to your wp-content/themes Folder
- Go to WP-Admin/Design and activate WP-Creativix
- Go to Design/Theme Settings and adjust to your needs
- To add Portfolio and Blog page go to "write page" and select "Blog" or "Portfolio" as Template
- To show images on Frontpage and Slideshow add a Custom Field with key "Featured" and value direct link to image (e.g. http://www.google.de/logo.png)
- To show images for Articles in Blog Layout add Custom Field with key "Blog" and value a direct link to image (e.g. http://www.google./logo.png)
